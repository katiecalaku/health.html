The Importance of Mental Health and Self Care

Why Is It Important?

Mental Health is a notion that at times in cultures can be considered of less importance, when in fact it is something that is just as important as our physical health.

If you are personally struggling with mental health there are some easy ways to start out to enforce it into your daily life:

  • Take 10 minutes a day to reflect back on the positive parts of your day.
  • Have something to look forward to everyday.
  • Do not feel guilty to treat yourself often!
  • Put yourself first.
  • Remember your strengths and apply them everywhere.

Daily Self Care Ideas!

  • Binge watch Netlfix or Youtube videos
  • Take a quick nap or bath!
  • Have a dance party!
